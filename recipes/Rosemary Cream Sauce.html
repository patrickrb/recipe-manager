Rosemary Cream Sauce

Cream Based, Sauce

Source: ChatGPT

Ingredients

Ingredients:


2 tablespoons unsalted butter


1 tablespoon olive oil


2 garlic cloves, minced


1 teaspoon fresh rosemary, finely chopped (or ½ teaspoon dried rosemary)


1 cup heavy cream


½ cup chicken or vegetable broth


½ cup grated Parmesan cheese


Salt and pepper to taste


Optional: A pinch of nutmeg for extra depth

Directions


1. Heat Butter and Oil:

In a medium saucepan or skillet, melt the butter with olive oil over medium heat.



2. Cook Garlic and Rosemary:

Add the minced garlic and rosemary. Cook for about 1-2 minutes until fragrant, being careful not to burn the garlic.



3. Add Liquid Ingredients:

Pour in the heavy cream and broth. Stir to combine, and bring the mixture to a gentle simmer.



4. Incorporate Parmesan:

Slowly stir in the Parmesan cheese until it melts and thickens the sauce.



5. Season:

Taste the sauce and season with salt, pepper, and a pinch of nutmeg if desired.



6. Simmer:

Let the sauce simmer for 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until it thickens to your desired consistency
