Mexican El Diablo

Drink, Smuggler's Cove, Tiki

Source: Smuggler's Cove

Ingredients

1 lime wedge

1/2 ounce fresh lime juice

1/2 ounce natural créme de cassis

1 1/2 ounces blanco tequila

4 ounces real ginger ale

Description

As Vic said of his 1946 invention, “I hate like hell to bring up unpleasant things at a time like this, but go easy on this one because it’s tough on your running board.”

SOURCE Trader Vic’s Book of Food and Drink, adapted by Smuggler’s Cove

GLASSWARE Collins or highball

Directions

GARNISH Swizzle stick

Squeeze and the drop lime wedge into a Collins or highball glass. Fill glass with cracked ice. Build the drink by adding the remaining ingredients; stir. Garnish with a swizzle stick.
