Southwest Chile Cheese Fondue

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 25 min Servings: 8 Source: pinterest.co.uk

Ingredients

Produce

2 tbsp Cilantro leaves and/or scallions

1 Garlic clove, large

2 Jalapeno or serrano chile peppers

1 Onion, medium

1 Red bell pepper

2 Tomatoes

Condiments

1 tbsp Lime juice, fresh

Baking & Spices

1/8 tsp Cayenne pepper

1 tbsp Cornstarch

1 Salt

Oils & Vinegars

1 1/2 tbsp Vegetable oil

Nuts & Seeds

1 tsp Cumin, ground

Dairy

8 oz Cheddar cheese, sharp

6 oz Cream cheese

2 cups Light cream or half-and-half

8 oz Monterey jack cheese
