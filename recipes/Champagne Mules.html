Champagne Mules

Champagne, Drink, Vodka

Prep Time: 5 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: Yields: 2 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

2 oz. vodka

2 oz. fresh lime juice

4 oz. ginger beer

Chilled champagne (or prosecco), for topping

Lime wedges, for serving

mint, for serving

Directions

Pour 1 ounce each vodka and fresh lime juice into two glasses, then top each glass with 2 ounces ginger beer. Pour champagne over then garnish with limes and mint.

Serve cold.
