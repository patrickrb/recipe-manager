Ranch Dressing

Salad

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: Yields: 1 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

2 large egg yolks

Juice of 1/2 a lemon

1 tsp. dijon mustard

1 c. light olive oil

3 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 c. freshly chopped parsley

2 tbsp. freshly chopped dill

2 tbsp. freshly chopped chives

2 tsp. kosher salt

1 tsp. onion powder

3/4 tsp. freshly ground black pepper

Pinch cayenne

1/4 c. almond milk

Directions

In a medium bowl, whisk together yolks, lemon juice, and mustard. While whisking add a few drops of oil at a time, then as it starts to thicken, add oil in a slow steady stream.

Stir in garlic, parsley, dill, and chives. Season with salt, pepper, onion powder, and a pinch of cayenne, then add milk. If ranch is too thick, add milk a tablespoon at a time to thin.
