Crab Rangoon Dip

Appetizers, Asian, Chinese

Prep Time: 10 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: Yields: 6 - 8 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

12 oz. cream cheese, softened

1/2 c. sour cream

1 c. shredded Monterey Jack

2 tsp. low-sodium soy sauce

2 tsp. sriracha

1 tsp. garlic powder

6 oz. lump crab

3 green onions, sliced

Pita chips, for serving

Directions

Preheat oven to 350°. In a large bowl, combine cream cheese, sour cream, Monterey Jack, soy sauce, Sriracha, and garlic powder. Beat until evenly combined. Fold in crab and green onions.

Transfer dip to a small baking dish. Bake for 20-25 minutes, until the dip is bubbly and golden.

Garnish with more green onions and drizzle with Sriracha, if desired. Serve warm with pita chips.
