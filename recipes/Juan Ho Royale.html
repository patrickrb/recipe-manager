Juan Ho Royale

Champagne, Drink, Smuggler's Cove, Tequila, Tiki

Source: Smuggler's Cove

Ingredients

2 ounces champagne or sparkling wine

3/4 ounce fresh lime juice

1/2 ounce SC Orgeat (page 330)

1/2 ounce blue curacao

1/2 ounce John D. Taylor’s Velvet Falernum

1 1/2 ounces tequila blanco

Description

Created for Hula’s in Santa Cruz, and originally named The Steamer after Steamer Lane, a famous surf spot in Santa Cruz. Hula’s wisely realized the name conjured a different image and cleverly renamed it Juan Ho. To capture the azure seas, I make a rare exception for blue curacao. Revisiting it again recently revealed that it benefitted nicely from a splash of bubbly.

ORIGIN Created by Martin Cate GLASSWARE Chilled coupe

Directions

GARNISH None

Pour the champagne into a chilled coupe. Add the remaining ingredients to a cocktail shaker and shake with cracked or cubed ice. Double-strain into the chilled coupe.
