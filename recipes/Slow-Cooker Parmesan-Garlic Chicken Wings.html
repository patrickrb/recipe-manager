Slow-Cooker Parmesan-Garlic Chicken Wings

★★★★★

Chicken, Game Day Food, Slow Cooker / Crockpot, Wings

Prep Time: 0 Cook Time: 3 Servings: 6 - 8 Source: delish.com

Ingredients

2 lb. chicken wings

1/2 stick butter, melted

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 tbsp. Dried Parsley

1 1/2 c. freshly grated Parmesan, divided

kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

Directions

Place chicken wings in a large slow-cooker.

In a large bowl, mix together butter, garlic, parsley, and 1 cup Parmesan and season with salt and pepper. Pour mixture over chicken wings and stir to coat.

Cover and cook on high until cooked through, 2 1/2 to 3 hours.

Heat broiler. Line two baking sheets with parchment paper and pour wings onto them. Sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Parmesan and broil until crispy, 5 minutes.
