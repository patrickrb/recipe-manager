Chinese Sticky Ribs

Asian, Chinese, Pork, Ribs

Source: Reddit.com

Ingredients

1 rack of pork ribs with membrane removed

1oz oil Salt/pepper

3oz ginger thinly sliced

6 garlic cloves thinly sliced

1-2oz tein tsin chiles chopped

6 star anise

1 tbsp Szechwan peppercorns

2oz rice wine

2oz palm sugar

3oz honey

3oz dark soy sauce

3oz rice vinegar

4 whites of green onion chopped

20oz beef stock

Directions

Break rack down into 2-3 bone segments
Season with salt and pepper.
Sear in oil until crust forms.
Add garlic and ginger
Add star anise, chile,and peppercorns
Deglaze with wine
Add sugar, honey, soy sauce, vinegar, green onion, and stock.
Cover with foil and braise 1-1 1/2 hours at 350 or until tender.
Drain liquid into pan and cook until reduced.
Add ribs and toss in glaze.
Serve with chopped green onion and rice.
