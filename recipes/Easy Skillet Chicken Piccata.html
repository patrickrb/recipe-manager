Easy Skillet Chicken Piccata

Chicken, Italian

Prep Time: 15 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: Yields: 4 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

1 tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil

4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs

Kosher salt

Freshly ground black pepper

2 tbsp. butter

3 cloves garlic, minced

1/4 c. dry white wine

Juice of 1 lemon

2 tbsp. capers

1 lemon, sliced

Freshly chopped parsley, for garnish

Directions

In a large ovenproof skillet over medium-high heat, heat oil. Season chicken with salt and pepper and cook until golden and no longer pink, 8 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate. Discard half the chicken juice from skillet and reduce heat to low.

To skillet, add butter, garlic, white wine, lemon juice, and capers and bring to a simmer. Add lemon slices and return chicken thighs to skillet. Let chicken simmer in sauce 5 minutes, then garnish with parsley before serving.
