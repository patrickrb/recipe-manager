Drink, Smuggler's Cove, Tiki

Source: Smuggler's Cove

Ingredients

1/4 ounce SC Cinnamon Syrup (page 327)

1/2 ounce Punt e Mes

1/4 ounce tawny port

2 ounces blended aged rum (3)

6 drops Bittermens ‘Elemakule Tiki bitters

Description

ORIGIN Created by Dane Barca

GLASSWARE Chilled coupe

Directions

GARNISH Maraschino cherry

Add all the ingredients to a mixing glass. Fill with cracked or cubed ice. Stir and strain into a chilled coupe. Float a maraschino cherry in the center for garnish.
