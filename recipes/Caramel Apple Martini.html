Caramel Apple Martini

Drink, Martini

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 5 mins Servings: Servings: 1 drink calories: 240kcal author: linda Source: Thewanderlustkitchen.com

Ingredients

2 oz vodka

1 oz sour apple schnapps

1/2 oz butterscotch schnapps

ice

caramel drizzle - optional garnish

Directions

If desired, prepare your martini glass with a caramel rim and caramel sauce at the bottom of the glass.

Then, in your cocktail shaker filled with ice, combine your vodka, sour apple schnapps, and butterscotch schnapps.

Next, close your shaker and shake vigorously for 20-30 seconds.

Finally, pour your martini into your prepared glass and enjoy!
