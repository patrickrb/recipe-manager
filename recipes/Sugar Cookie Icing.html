Sugar Cookie Icing

Christmas, Cookies, Dessert, Frosting

Prep Time: 5 Cook Time: 0 hours Servings: Yields: 1 Source: Delish.com

Ingredients

3 c. powdered sugar

1/4 c. light corn syrup

1/4 c. milk, plus more for thinning

1/4 tsp. almond (or pure vanilla) extract

Directions

In a medium bowl stir together powdered sugar, corn syrup, milk, and almond extract. Add milk a teaspoon at a time to thin, if necessary.
