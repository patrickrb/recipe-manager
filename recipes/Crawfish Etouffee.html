Crawfish Etouffee

Cajun, Crawfish, Seafood, Spicy, Winter Food

Prep Time: 20 minutes Cook Time: 1 hour Total Time: 1hour 20 minutes Difficulty: Easy Servings: 8 Source: Mom

Ingredients

2 Cups Chopped Onion

1/2 Cup Celery Chopped

2 lbs peeled crawfish tails

1 10 3/4 oz can cream of mushroom sopu

2 bay leaf

6 cloves of garlic, minced

1/2 green pepper, chopped

1/2 cup butter

1 16oz can of tomato sauce

3/4 Cup of water

3 teaspoons liquid crab boil

Juice from 1/2 a lemon


Green onion for topping

Directions

1) In a heavy saucepan saute onion bell pepper & celery in butter and cook over medium heat stirring often until vegetables are soft
2) In a bowl, season crawfish with red pepper, black pepper, salt, and lemon juice
3) Add crawfish to the onion mixture and cook uncovered for 5 minutes
4) Add tomato sauce, mushroom soup, water, bay leaf, crab boil and garlic.
5) Cover and simmer for 30 minutes
6 Server over hot rice and sprinkle with green onion
