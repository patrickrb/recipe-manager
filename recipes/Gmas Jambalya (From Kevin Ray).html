Gmas Jambalya (From Kevin Ray)

★★★★★

Cajun, Chicken, Rice, Sausage

Prep Time: 30 minutes Cook Time: 2 hours

Ingredients

3 lbs large link sausage

5 lbs pre-cooked chicken

4 cups raw rice

2 stick of butter (melted)

1/2 cup chopped green onion

8 cloves garlic

48oz beef broth

2 cans French onion soup

2 small cans tomato sauce

Salt

Pepper

1 chopped onion

1 chopped bell pepper


Directions

Combine all ingredients and place into 2 11x13 pans

Cover and bake at 350 for 2 hours
