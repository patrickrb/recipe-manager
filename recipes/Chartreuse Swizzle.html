Chartreuse Swizzle

Drink, Smuggler's Cove, Tiki

Source: Smuggler's Cove

Ingredients

1 ounce pineapple juice

3/4 ounce fresh lime juice

1/2 ounce John D. Taylor's Velvet Falernum

1 1/2 ounces Green Chartreuse

Description

While it’s been a staple of the cocktail renaissance for years now, the structure of this drink owes a debt to the exotic cocktail tradition. Sour, sweet, strong. And if the falernum isn't enough spice for you, how about a hundred or so more in the mix from the Chartreuse?

ORIGIN Created by Marcovaldo Dionysos GLASSWARE Collins or Zombie glass

Directions

GARNISH Swizzle napkin wrap (see page 245), freshly grated nutmeg and mint sprig.

Combine all the ingredients in a Collins or Zombie giass. Fill the glass with crushed ice until it is threequarters full. Swizzle with a lélé or barspoon. Top up with additional crushed ice as needed to fill the glass. Add garnish.
