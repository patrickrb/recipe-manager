Witchy Woman

Drink, Mocktail, Rum, Tequila

Prep Time: 20 min Cook Time: 20 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: Yield: 1 mocktail Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

For the Blueberry-Sage Syrup:

1 cup sugar

3/4 cup water

1/2 cup blueberries

1/2 cup sage leaves

For the Drink:

2 ounces distilled nonalcoholic spirits (such as Ritual Zero-Proof Tequila Alternative)

3/4 ounce Lyre’s Orange Sec

1/2 ounce fresh lime juice

4 dashes of orange bitters

Merlot salt, for garnish

Edible flower, dehydrated lime wheel and/or sage leaf, for garnish

Directions

Make the blueberry-sage syrup: Combine the sugar, water and blueberries in a medium saucepan over medium-high heat. Bring to a simmer, stirring. Muddle the berries well, mashing them in the pan. Add the sage and simmer 2 to 3 minutes. Let cool 10 minutes, then strain through a fine-mesh sieve, pressing on the solids. Refrigerate until cooled, about 1 hour.

Make the drink: Combine the spirits, orange sec, lime juice, bitters and 3/4 ounce of the blueberry-sage syrup in a cocktail shaker with ice. Shake well.

Strain the drink into a rocks glass (either rim the glass with merlot salt or sprinkle some on top for garnish). Add ice. Garnish with a flower, lime wheel or sage leaf.
