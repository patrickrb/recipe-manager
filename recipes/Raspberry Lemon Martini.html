Raspberry Lemon Martini

★★★★

Drink, Martini

Ingredients

3 oz vodka (plain or citrus-flavored)


¾ oz fresh lemon juice


1 oz raspberry syrup


¼ oz triple sec (optional, for a hint of orange depth)


Ice

Description

Flavor: Bright, fruity, and refreshing

Directions

1. Add all ingredients to a shaker with ice.

2. Shake hard for 10–15 seconds.

3. Strain into a chilled martini glass.

4. Garnish with a few fresh raspberries or a lemon twist.
