Sparkling Mai Tai

Champagne, Drink, Rum, Smuggler's Cove, Tiki

Source: Smuggler's Cove

Ingredients

1/4 ounce fresh lime juice

1/4 ounce SC Orgeat (page 330)

1/2 ounce Pierre Ferrand Dry Curasao

1/4 ounce black blended overproof rum (6)

1/2 ounce blended aged rum (3)

4 ounces chilled sparkling wine

Description

ORIGIN Created by Martin Cate source Created for Sunset magazine, 2011
GLASSWARE Champagne flute or coupe

Directions

GARNISH Lime twist and mint leaf.

Pour all the ingredients except the sparking wine into a mixing glass. Stir with cracked or cubed ice. Strain into a chilled champagne flute or coupe and top with sparkling wine, then garnish.

Notes

NOTE To save time, you can batch this recipe ahead of time. To serve several people, multiply the ingredient quantities by the number of guests. Combine all the ingredients except the sparkling wine and chill for an hour. When youre ready to serve, pour 194 ounces of the chilled mix into each glass (no need to stir with ice) and top each with 4 ounces of chilled sparkling wine.
