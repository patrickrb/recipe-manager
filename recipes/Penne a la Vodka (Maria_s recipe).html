Penne a la Vodka (Maria's recipe)

Italian

Ingredients

2 tsp olive oil

2 cloves garlic, minced

1 (28 oz) can whole peed tomatoes - we just use crushed so you don't need a blender

1/2 cups chopped fresh basil

salt & pepper to taste

1/4 cup vodka

1 pound penne pasta (or rigatoni)

1 pint heavy cream

Directions

1. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook garlic in olive oil until tender, 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in tomatoes, breaking up a bit with a fork. Stir in basil, salt & pepper & simmer 15 minutes. Stir in vodka & cook 15 minutes more.
2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add pasta and cook for 8-10 minutes or until al dente; drain.
3. Stir cream into sauce and cook 10 minutes more. Toss with hot pasta.

* You can add chopped prosciutto when you add in the heavy cream.
