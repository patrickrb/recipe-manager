Bread Machine Naan

Bread, Bread Machine

Prep Time: 1 hr 55 min Cook Time: prep: 1-1/2 hours. cook: 5 min./batch Servings: 6 servings Source: Tasteofhome.com

Ingredients

3/4 cup warm 2% milk (70° to 80°)

3/4 cup plain yogurt

1 large egg, room temperature, beaten

2 tablespoons canola oil

2 teaspoons sugar

1 teaspoon salt

1 teaspoon baking powder

4 cups bread flour

2 teaspoons active dry yeast

Directions

Chewy yeast-raised flat bread is a snap to make in a bread machine. Serve naan with your favorite Indian dish to soak up the curry and sauces. —Shannon Ventresca, Middleboro, Massachusetts
